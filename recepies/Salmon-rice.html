Salmon with rice and stemmed broccoli

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Seasoning (anything can work, try soy sauce)
  • Rice
  • Stemmed broccoli

Steps

  1. Season Salmon and place in an oven preheated to 200c for 20 mins
  2. Season broccoli and add after 5 mins
  3. Cook rice (takes about 15mins - bring to boil, place on 4 for
    10 mins then off heat for 10 mins)
