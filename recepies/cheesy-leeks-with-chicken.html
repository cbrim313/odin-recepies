Cheesy Leeks with Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Leeks
  • Blue Cheese
  • Soft Cheese
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into chunks and fry off
  2. Fry off the leeks until soft
  3. Add 300ml of chicken stock, bring to the boil
    then leave to simmer until reduced
  4. add the 2 cheeses and mix, add the chicken back into the pan
